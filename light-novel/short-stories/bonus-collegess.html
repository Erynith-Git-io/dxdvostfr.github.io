In Hyoudou Residence...

"What's up, you two?"

"Ah, we were discussing about which club we would be joining in college, now that we've familiarised ourselves with the environment."

A club, is it? I mean, they were college students, so it was only natural to become interested in joining a club. In high school, it was the Occult Research Club, so I too became interested in what kind of club they would be joining in college.

I asked as I looked at the pamphlet.

"Are you thinking of joining a cultural club, like in high school, or a sports club? Or rather, it would be bad if you girls were to stand out in sports?"

Because their physical abilities were much higher than normal humans, I guessed it would be hard to enter a sports club. Well, they just need to hide it, but.....to hide it every time would be troublesome.

Looking at the pamphlet, my eyes stopped moving at the one marked with a pen.

─Occult Research Society!

There's one in the college too!? Oh, it's a [Society], not a [Club]. Oh, this feels so club-like!

"This one......it's not related to us, right?"

"Of course, it's not related. The Occult Club in high school was revived by us, so it's different from the one in the college."

I know, right? This means that this is a normal occult club, with normal people as their members.

"Ufufu, for the time being, I was talking to Rias about going on a tour at this Occult Research Society. I heard there are only women who enjoy the occult as club members."

I see, as expected, both of them had become curious about it. Or rather, a women-only club who loves occult definitely seemed amazing......
